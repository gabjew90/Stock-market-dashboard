# Track record — calls & outcomes

Notable market calls Dr. Wish made on the blog and how they turned out.

*Note: this page is thin. It will grow substantially when the Plan 2.5 timeline parser runs and extracts systematic signal data from the daily-update posts. The entries here come from teaching posts where he documents a specific call and its outcome in retrospect.*

---

## 2000 — Exit on 30-week average reversal

Dr. Wish credits the reversal of the QQQQ's 30-week moving average downward in **2000** as the signal that took him out of the market before the dot-com crash. ([WW 2005-07-17](../../raw/posts/2005-07-17-gmi-since-inception-introducing-the-wpm-on-analyst-earnings-estimates-ibd-100-rockets.md))

**Outcome:** the Nasdaq Composite fell approximately 78% from its March 2000 peak to its October 2002 trough. He described himself (April 2005) as having "successfully dodged the 98 bear, the 2000-2002 bear and most of the present decline."

---

## 2003 — Re-entry on 30-week average reversal

The reversal of the QQQQ's 30-week average back upward in **2003** signalled re-entry. ([WW 2005-07-17](../../raw/posts/2005-07-17-gmi-since-inception-introducing-the-wpm-on-analyst-earnings-estimates-ibd-100-rockets.md))

**Outcome:** the QQQQ roughly tripled from the 2003 bottom to the 2007 peak.

---

## 1995–2000 — IRA multiplied 14-fold

During the 1995–2000 Nasdaq bull run (index up ~584%), Dr. Wish multiplied his IRA **14-fold** using Darvas/O'Neil-inspired growth-stock techniques. ([WW 2005-10-09](../../raw/posts/2005-10-09-nicolas-darvas-trading-techniques-require-markets-at-all-time-peaks.md))

---

## November 2021 — Exit at QQQ peak on breadth divergence

**The call:** In November 2021, while QQQ was still at all-time highs (day U-26 of the short-term up-trend), Dr. Wish noticed that new lows in his US stock universe were surging. On **11/22/2021** — which proved to be QQQ's exact peak — he recorded 438 new lows while QQQ was at an ATH. He exited the market. ([WW 2023-06-19](../../raw/posts/2023-06-19-blog-post-here-is-an-explanation-of-how-i-compute-new-us-highs-and-lows-each-day-and-used-it-to-exit-in-11-20.md))

**Signal used:** breadth divergence — surging new lows while the index was at highs. Not the GMI (which can remain GREEN longer). He describes this as a "lucky call" but attributes it to the divergence signal.

**Outcome:** QQQ entered a Stage 4 decline after the November 2021 peak. He stayed out of the market for over a year.
