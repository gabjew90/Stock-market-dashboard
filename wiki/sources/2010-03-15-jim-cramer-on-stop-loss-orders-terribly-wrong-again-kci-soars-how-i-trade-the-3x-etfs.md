# WW 2010-03-15 — Jim Cramer on stop loss orders; how I trade the 3X ETFs

**Source:** [Jim Cramer on stop loss orders–terribly wrong again! KCI soars; How I trade the 3X ETF's](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2010/03/jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Dr. Wish responds to Jim Cramer's claim that part-time traders should not use stop losses, arguing the opposite: part-time traders are *most* in need of automatic stop-loss orders precisely because they are not watching the market continuously. He explains his stop-loss discipline in detail: how he places stops, when he uses GTC (good-til-cancelled) orders, how he adjusts stops as a position profits, and his approach to trading 3X leveraged ETFs. He credits Nicolas Darvas's use of stops as a key element of his success.

## Key claims

- Part-time traders need automatic stop-loss orders **more** than professionals who watch every tick. The cost of not using them can be catastrophic (he cites a personal loss from a TASR trade in 2005). ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- Stop placement: decided **before the buy**, based on prior support, a moving average, or a recent reaction low. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- As soon as a buy order fills, he immediately places a GTC stop at the predetermined exit price. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- He does **not** use automatic trailing stops — he raises stops manually after reviewing technicals. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- Stops should be **raised** as a position profits, so that a new stop ensures not all profit is given back. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- He will buy back a stock at a higher price after being stopped out, if the trend resumes. "Every small loss brings me to my next big gain." ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- 3X ETF tactic: buy a **small initial position** and add only if the ETF moves up (average up, not down). Prefers to enter at the start of a QQQ up-trend. Example: TYH rose 15.5% vs QQQ's 6.2% in the same 16-day up-trend. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
- T2108 at 84% was noted as "not far from the highest levels it gets to" — a reason to move stops up. ([WW 2010-03-15](../../raw/posts/2010-03-15-jim-cramer-on-stop-loss-orders-terribly-wrong-again-kci-soars-how-i-trade-the-3x-etfs.md))
