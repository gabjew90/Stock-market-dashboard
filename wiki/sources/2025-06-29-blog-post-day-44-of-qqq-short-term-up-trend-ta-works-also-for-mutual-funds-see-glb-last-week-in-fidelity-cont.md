# WW 2025-06-29 — GLB applied to mutual funds; 10wk/30wk crossover for pension; Guppy weekly RWB

**Source:** [Blog Post: Day 44 of $QQQ short term up-trend; TA works also for mutual funds; GLB in Fidelity Contrafund; limit losses](../../raw/posts/2025-06-29-blog-post-day-44-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-ta-works-also-for-mutual-funds-see-glb-last-week-in-fidelity-cont.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2025/06/blog-post-day-44-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-ta-works-also-for-mutual-funds-see-glb-last-week-in-fidelity-cont/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

A June 2025 post demonstrating that the GLB concept and the 10wk/30wk crossover rule apply to mutual funds, not just stocks and ETFs. Fidelity Contrafund (FCNKX) had a GLB on its weekly chart. The 10:30 weekly chart shows: when the 10-week average (dotted) crosses above the 30-week average (solid red), it can signal a major up-trend — the reverse signals a down-trend. Dr. Wish had been slowly transferring cash from money market funds to mutual funds and stocks as QQQ and SPY showed their 10-week averages crossing above their 30-week averages. The modified weekly Guppy chart of QQQ confirmed a weekly RWB up-trend. GMI=6 at time of writing. Dr. Wish describes his investment philosophy as reacting to market action rather than news, and always limiting losses.

## Key claims

- The GLB concept works on mutual fund monthly charts (FCNKX demonstrated June 2025).
- The 10:30 weekly chart crossover rule is the pension-account signal: 10wk avg crossing above 30wk avg = major up-trend; crossing below = major down-trend.
- The weekly Guppy chart of QQQ confirmed a weekly RWB up-trend by late June 2025.
- "Always limiting losses" is cited as the essential practice for long-run survival — not market prediction.
- Dr. Wish re-stated his approach: "I focus on the market's movement and ignore the news and mental masturbation of the many media pundits."
