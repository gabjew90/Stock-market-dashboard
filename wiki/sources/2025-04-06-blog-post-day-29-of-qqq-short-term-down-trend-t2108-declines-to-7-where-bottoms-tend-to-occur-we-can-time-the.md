# WW 2025-04-06 — T2108 at 7%; 10wk/30wk cross down; market timing is possible

**Source:** [Blog Post: Day 29 of $QQQ short term down-trend; T2108 declines to 7%, where bottoms tend to occur; We CAN time the market](../../raw/posts/2025-04-06-blog-post-day-29-of-qqq-short-term-down-trend-t2108-declines-to-7-where-bottoms-tend-to-occur-we-can-time-the.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2025/04/blog-post-day-29-of-qqq-short-term-down-trend-t2108-declines-to-7-where-bottoms-tend-to-occur-we-can-time-the/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

An April 2025 post during a sharp decline (tariff-driven). QQQ's 10-week average crossed below its 30-week average for the first time since February 2023 — Dr. Wish notes this "usually happens at the beginning of a major decline." Simultaneously, T2108 had fallen to 7%, an extreme oversold level where prior bottoms have tended to occur. SQQQ (bought on Day 1 of the down-trend) was up +65% from Day 1. He is "almost all in cash" and will not rush back in on the first rally — waiting for signs of a new up-trend. He explicitly defends the possibility of market timing, listing the declines he avoided since 2000 using Stage Analysis, GMI, Guppy charts, and 10:30 weekly charts.

## Key claims

- QQQ's 10-week average crossing below its 30-week average is a serious warning — it "usually happens at the beginning of a major decline" (corroborated April 2025).
- T2108 at 7% is in the zone where bottoms "tend to occur" — not a guaranteed bottom, but historically meaningful.
- The appropriate response at T2108 ~7% is patience, not panic buying — wait for evidence of a new up-trend before re-entering.
- Market timing IS possible using Stage Analysis + GMI + Guppy + 10:30 weekly charts; Dr. Wish cites avoidance of 2000, 2008, 2020, and 2022 declines as proof.
- SQQQ held from Day 1 of a down-trend can generate outsized gains if the trend persists (+65% here).
