# WW 2026-03-15 — GMI=0 and Red; Weinstein Stage 4 onset; T2108 at 23%

## What it covers

A March 2026 post describing his defensive stance with GMI=0 and Red, more 52-week lows than highs, and the 10-week average now declining — a possible beginning of a Weinstein Stage 4 down-trend. QQQ's 10-week average had been rising above the 30-week for 38 straight weeks before the breakdown. T2108 was at 23%, still far from the below-10% level that signals a bottom. He is "safely in cash." He reiterates the historical efficacy of the 30-week average signal to exit all major declines since 2000. The post also repeats his rule for T2108 below 10%: buy a little SPY, accumulate only on the way up at higher prices.

## Key claims

- The 10-week average declining below the 30-week average marks a possible Stage 4 onset — the same signal that triggered exits in 2000, 2008, 2020, and 2022.
- T2108 at 23% is not yet a bottom signal; the threshold is below 10% for the extreme-oversold contrarian buy.
- When T2108 eventually drops below 10%: buy SPY (not individual stocks), starting small, accumulate only on the way up.
- Above-average weekly volume on recent declines confirms institutional selling, not just retail noise.
- In a Stage 4 decline, the index sometimes bounces to kiss the 10-week or even 30-week average — that rebound can be a good time to sell remaining longs if one missed the earlier exit.
