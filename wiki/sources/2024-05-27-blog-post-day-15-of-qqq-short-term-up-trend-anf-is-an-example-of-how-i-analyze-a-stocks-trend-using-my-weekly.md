# WW 2024-05-27 — ANF as a worked example of the weekly green bar indicator

**Source:** [Blog Post: Day 15 of $QQQ short term up-trend; $ANF is an example of how I analyze a stock's trend using my weekly green bar indicator.](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2024/05/blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly-green-bar-indicator/) · tier: trade_example

## What it covers

Dr. Wish explains and demonstrates his **weekly green bar** scan and the specific indicators on his weekly chart: the 4-week, 10-week, and 30-week moving averages, and the weekly 10.4 / 10.4.4 stochastics. He uses ANF as a worked example of a stock that sustained an up-trend from mid-June 2023 to April 2024 — never closing below the 10-week average in that period, and giving 20+ green-bar bounce setups off the 4-week average. He also shows why he prefers weekly charts over daily charts (fewer shakeouts) and explains the scan criteria mechanically.

## Key claims

- The weekly chart is preferred over the daily chart because daily moves "shake me out of a good up-trending stock." Weekly bars absorb intra-week noise. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- Weekly chart indicators used: 4wk (red dot), 10wk (blue dot), 30wk (solid red) moving averages + weekly 10.4 and 10.4.4 stochastics. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- Trend prerequisite: **4wk > 10wk > 30wk** — all three averages must be aligned in that order. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- A **weekly stochastic above 80** is not an overbought condition for an advancing stock — it means the current price is above 80% of the range of the past 10 weeks, which is what you want to see. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- **Weekly green bar scan criteria** (all must be met): (1) weekly 10.4 stochastic above 80 for at least 5 weeks; (2) 4wk > 10wk > 30wk; (3) stock up at least 50% from a year ago; (4) reached a recent ATH; (5) had a green bar the most recent week. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- **Exit rules:** sell if the stock closes the week below the 10-week average, OR if the 4-week average declines below the 10-week average. Also may sell if the stock trades below the low of the most recent green bar (usable as a stop). ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- ANF traced the pattern from mid-June 2023 to April 2024 — 4wk>10wk>30wk the entire time; never closed below the 10-week average; 10.4 stochastic above 80 continuously until the April 2024 mini-correction. The 4-week average gave 20+ green-bar entries vs the 10-week average's 4 bounces. ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
- This weekly green bar setup requires an up-trending market (GMI = GREEN and a current QQQ short-term up-trend). ([WW 2024-05-27](../../raw/posts/2024-05-27-blog-post-day-15-of-qqq-short-term-up-trend-anf-is-an-example-of-how-i-analyze-a-stocks-trend-using-my-weekly.md))
