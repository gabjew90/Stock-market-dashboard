# WW 2022-06-05 — GMI Green vs Red performance history; ATH-only stock selection

**Source:** [Day 37 of $QQQ short term down-trend; 14 IBD/MarketSmith stocks at 20 year highs; How GMI signals have worked](../../raw/posts/2022-06-05-bog-post-day-37-of-qqq-short-term-down-trend-14-ibd-marketsmith-stocks-at-20-year-high-on-friday.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2022/06/bog-post-day-37-of-qqq-short-term-down-trend-14-ibd-marketsmith-stocks-at-20-year-high-on-friday/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Dr. Wish presents charts showing GMI buy/sell periods overlaid on QQQ price history, demonstrating how his methodology avoids the worst declines. He also explains his ATH-first stock selection philosophy: the biggest winners break to an ATH, consolidate, then break to a new ATH. He shows 14 stocks from his IBD/MS watchlist hitting ATH during a GMI Red period — all from commodity and oil sectors. The GLB re-entry-after-failure rule is stated explicitly.

## Key claims

- **ATH-only stock selection:** "I draw a green horizontal line on a monthly chart at the top bar that has not been exceeded for at least 3 subsequent bars (3 months)... I like to buy stocks when they close above their green line." If he misses the breakout, he only buys stocks trading *above* their last green line when they bounce off support. ([WW 2022-06-05](../../raw/posts/2022-06-05-bog-post-day-37-of-qqq-short-term-down-trend-14-ibd-marketsmith-stocks-at-20-year-high-on-friday.md))
- **GLB re-entry after failure:** "If it fails I often repurchase it upon the next GLB." A failed first GLB (stock closes back below the green line) does not disqualify the stock permanently.
- **Darvas/Ryan criteria:** "Darvas liked to buy stocks that had already doubled from a year earlier. David Ryan said that he likes to buy doublers too." His table sorts by close/close 250 days ago.
- **GMI Red period evidence (2022):** 14 of 782 watchlist stocks still at ATH in a GMI=1 environment; all from oil/gas. "It is so much easier to make $$$ when the GMI is Green and registering 5 or 6."
- **IBD/MS watchlist filter:** stocks must be from his watchlist of stocks *recently mentioned* by IBD or MarketSmith services.
- GMI Green vs Red QQQ charts shared visually — GMI Red periods clearly correspond to the largest drawdowns.
