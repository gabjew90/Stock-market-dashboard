# WW 2018-11-25 — I'm back! Daily BWR pattern for $QQQ, weekly RWB pattern gone

**Source:** [I'm back! Daily BWR pattern for $QQQ, weekly RWB pattern gone–content to be on sidelines](../../raw/posts/2018-11-25-im-back-daily-bwr-pattern-for-qqq-weekly-rwb-pattern-gone-content-to-be-on-sidelines.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2018/11/im-back-daily-bwr-pattern-for-qqq-weekly-rwb-pattern-gone-content-to-be-on-sidelines/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Written after a personal absence (death of a sibling), this post is Dr. Wish's clearest statement of the BWR pattern as the bearish inverse of RWB. He shows the QQQ's **daily** Guppy chart has turned BWR (shorter red EMAs below the longer blue EMAs), while the **weekly** Guppy chart has also lost its prior RWB alignment. He uses this to explain why he is comfortably in cash. He also discusses the GMI (at 0), T2108 (recovered to 28% after an intraday low of 7.6% on Oct 26), and the investment-letter sentiment poll as a contrarian indicator.

## Key claims

- The daily Guppy chart shows a clear BWR down-trend for QQQ in November 2018: shorter red averages below the longer blue averages.
- The weekly Guppy chart had shown a strong RWB up-trend for two years; that pattern is now gone (white separation between red and blue lines has vanished).
- The GMI flashed Red on October 8, 2018 — Dr. Wish was already in cash in his university pension and moved his IRA to cash quickly after.
- A constriction and leveling off of the blue lines (as happened the prior April/May) may be the first sign of a developing bottom — but he does not act on that alone.
- He looks for: GMI returning to 4 or more for two consecutive days AND the daily Guppy chart turning from BWR to RWB before going long.
- Investment-letter sentiment poll at 40% bulls (down from 60%+ near the top) is promising as a contrarian indicator; 20% bears is still too few for a major bottom.
- T2108 hit an intraday low of 7.6% on October 26, 2018 — consistent with the oversold-bouncing zone. Recovered to 28% at the time of the post.
