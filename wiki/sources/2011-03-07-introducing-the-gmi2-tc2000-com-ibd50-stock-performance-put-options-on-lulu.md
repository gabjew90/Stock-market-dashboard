# WW 2011-03-07 — Introducing the GMI2

**Source:** [Introducing the GMI2; TC2000.com; IBD50 stock performance: put options on LULU](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2011/03/introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Dr. Wish announces the GMI2, which replaces the GMI-R. He explains the structure of both: the GMI has 6 indicators; the GMI-R added 4 indicators to the GMI's 6; the new GMI2 takes those 4 GMI-R indicators and adds 2 more, giving 6 GMI + 6 GMI2 = two separate 6-point composites. He states his decision rules for the GMI explicitly for the first time: long if GMI ≥ 4; defensive if GMI ≤ 3. He also introduces the T2108 reading and the QQQ short-term trend count in the context of his published weekly table.

## Key claims

- The GMI and GMI2 each count 6 separate indicators. ([WW 2011-03-07](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md))
- Explicit decision rule: "I like to be long if the GMI is 4 or more. When it declines to 3 or below, I get defensive in my trading IRA." ([WW 2011-03-07](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md))
- "I do not have decision rules based on the GMI-R or the new GMI2." The GMI2 is used for monitoring, not for triggering buys/sells. ([WW 2011-03-07](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md))
- The T2108 was at 70% at this time, with 80% described as a short-term top zone. ([WW 2011-03-07](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md))
- The QQQ short-term trend count was "U-1"; the prior up-trend had lasted 64 days. ([WW 2011-03-07](../../raw/posts/2011-03-07-introducing-the-gmi2-tc2000-com-ibd50-stock-performance-put-options-on-lulu.md))
- The GMI-R preceded the GMI2 — he does not define its exact 4 components here; he only says the GMI2 takes those 4 and adds 2 more. The component details are not disclosed in this post.

## Evolution note

The progression is: GMI (6 components, 2005) → GMI-R (GMI + 4 more = 10, later period) → GMI2 (new 6-component companion, 2011). The GMI itself did not change; it's the second indicator that evolved. See [gmi.md](../methodology/gmi.md) for the full evolution.
