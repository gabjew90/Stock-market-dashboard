# WW 2010-02-22 — How to use IBD 100 and New America stocks to find rocket stocks

**Source:** [How to use IBD 100 and New America stocks and TC2007 to find potential rocket stocks; Market rally begins](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2010/02/how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Written on the first day of a new QQQ short-term up-trend (U-1, GMI=6), Dr. Wish walks through his full stock-selection scan workflow: starting from the universe of new 52-week highs, filtering for stocks near their all-time high (past 10 years), applying fundamental criteria (quarterly earnings ≥30% or revenue growth ≥12%), and cross-referencing with the IBD 100 and New America lists. The post also attributes the "stocks at all-time highs" approach to Darvas and O'Neil.

## Key claims

- The prerequisite to searching for stocks is a GMI ≥ 3 (he says "at least 3"). He only buys long when the market's trend is up. ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- Darvas liked to buy stocks that were at all-time highs and had **already doubled in the past year**. O'Neil advocated the same. Both emphasized trading consistent with the market's trend. ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- Scan workflow: (1) sort universe of 4,000 stocks by new 52-week high; (2) inspect monthly charts to find those at or near their 10-year high; (3) flag those with earnings ≥ 30% OR revenue growth ≥ 12%; (4) cross-reference with IBD 100/New America watch lists. ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- Starting from 222 new 52-week highs, 32 were near their 10-year high; 22 survived the fundamental filter. Of those 22, 15 (68%) had independently appeared on an IBD 100 or New America list. ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- He notes that for most of the 22 stocks with ≥35% earnings growth, the PE was less than the earnings growth % — meaning PEG < 1, suggesting reasonable valuation despite high PE. ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- Personal rule: "Enter every trade as if it might fail so that I have an exit plan ready." ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
- Key lesson: "The key to profitable trading is to have many small losses and a few large gains." ([WW 2010-02-22](../../raw/posts/2010-02-22-how-to-use-ibd-100-and-new-america-stocks-and-tc2007-to-find-potential-rocket-stocks-market-rally-begun.md))
