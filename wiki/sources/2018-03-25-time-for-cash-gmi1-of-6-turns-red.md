# WW 2018-03-25 — GMI Red March 2018; put/call contrarian; 30-week curve-down pension trigger

**Source:** [Time for cash, GMI=1 (of 6), turns Red](../../raw/posts/2018-03-25-time-for-cash-gmi1-of-6-turns-red.md) · [original](https://wishingwealthblog.com/2018/03/time-for-cash-gmi1-of-6-turns-red/) · tier: teaching

## What it covers

Real-time post during the 2018 Q1 correction. GMI had just fallen to 1 and flashed a Red signal. Dr. Wish uses this moment to document several additional timing overlays he consults alongside the GMI: the put/call ratio (contrarian at extremes), the daily 10,4 stochastic (at 9 = very oversold), and the weekly RWB/BWR pattern on QQQ, SPY, and DIA. He articulates the precise trigger for pension exit: when the 30-week average itself **curves down** (not just when price crosses below it). Also introduces the "window dressing / sell-in-May / come-back-at-Halloween" seasonal pattern.

## Key claims

- GMI=1 (Friday, March 23, 2018) = Red signal; Day 1 of new QQQ short-term down-trend. About 40% of short-term down-trends last under 5 days.
- Put/call ratio at 1.25 on that Friday — extreme bearishness → contrarian indicator suggesting a short-term bounce up, though not necessarily a trend reversal. ([WW 2018-03-25](../../raw/posts/2018-03-25-time-for-cash-gmi1-of-6-turns-red.md))
- T2108 at 26%, but it had reached 8.6% in the February 2018 decline; below 10% = really good sign of oversold market.
- Daily stochastic 10,4 at 9 — historically the level at which index ETFs bounce short-term.
- The 30-week average for DIA and SPY had already curved below the QQQ's position; once the **30-week average curves down**, that is the trigger to exit the pension (university mutual funds). "That signal got me out before the 2000 and 2008 market debacles."
- He had not yet transferred pension to money market at the time of this post — waiting for the QQQ's 30-week average to curve down.
- Weekly Guppy RWB chart of the market shows the pattern had lasted since 2013 with one flat period in 2015-16; this may be the beginning of another such pause.
- Seasonal note: "Sell in May and go away and come back at Halloween" — may apply in 2018, or may not; he notes it without prescribing it.
