# WW 2025-03-30 — Day 24 of QQQ down-trend; $IWM at beginning of Stage 4

## What it covers

A current-day (March 2025) post in which Dr. Wish identifies IWM (Russell 2000) as beginning a Stage 4 decline, using its weekly 10:30 chart. He compares the current IWM chart to 2022 visually. He is in cash plus small positions in SQQQ and TWM (leveraged inverse ETFs). GMI has been Red since February 24. He articulates his approach for near-retirement investors versus young investors. He reiterates the "30 week average curving down = go to cash" rule.

## Key claims

- Stage 4 identification rule: when the 30-week average curves down (begins declining). In March 2025, IWM's 10-week average (dotted line) had crossed below the 30-week average (red line), and the weekly close (gray line) is leading everything down — he says this "looks like the beginning of a Stage 4."
- He compares IWM in March 2025 to IWM in 2022 (where he drew an arrow on the 2022 chart indicating the Stage 4 entry point, followed by a sustained decline).
- He distinguishes between young investors (can stay in SPY through declines; the market comes back in their lifetimes) and near-retirement investors (must protect capital; cannot wait for recovery).
- He transferred all his pension accumulations out of mutual funds weeks before this post. In 2000 he sold growth mutual funds at $100+ and watched them fall to the 30s; he reinvested when QQQ's 30-week curved back up and his account grew ~50% that year.
- GMI Red since February 24, 2025 as a secondary confirmation of the defensive stance.
- He holds SQQQ and TWM as small short positions when the market is in a down-trend.
